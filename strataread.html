Overview

StrataRead is built for reading and annotating PDFs with the assumption that your real thinking happens in notes and writing—Obsidian, StrataWrite, and other tools you already use.

Platforms: macOS & iPadOS

Focus: reading, annotation, and organizing PDFs for research workflows

Privacy: no tracking, no analytics; data stays on-device or in iCloud (if enabled)

Best for: researchers who annotate heavily and want to move highlights/notes into a thinking system

Works well with: Obsidian, StrataWrite, and other note-taking workflows

Support: stance@mac.com

How StrataRead Fits a Research Workflow

1) Read & annotate

  • Keep PDFs organized for rapid access during research and writing
  • Use annotations to capture arguments, methods, and quotable claims

2) Move insights into notes

  • Use StrataRead as the reading surface
  • Use your note system (Obsidian, StrataWrite) as the synthesis surface

Key Ideas

Annotation-first: StrataRead is designed around what matters in scholarly reading: marking up PDFs and extracting meaning.

Library-minded: Keep PDFs organized so you can find what you need quickly—especially during writing.

Apple-native: A SwiftUI app built on native Apple frameworks for a clean, responsive experience.

Calm by default: Minimal interface, fewer knobs, more reading.
