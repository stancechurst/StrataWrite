Overview

StrataFeed is designed for the “top of funnel” part of research: discovery, scanning, and deciding what is worth deeper reading. It helps you stay current and explore emerging directions before you commit time to full-text reading.

Platforms: macOS & iPadOS

Focus: discovery, triage, and exploration of scholarly literature

Data source: powered by OpenAlex

Best for: keeping up with new papers, scanning across fields, and exploring topics

Pairs with: StrataRead (deep reading) and StrataWrite (synthesis and drafting)

Support: stance@mac.com

How StrataFeed Fits a Research Workflow

1) Discover

  • Surface new papers relevant to your interests
  • Explore topics, authors, and trends over time

2) Triage

  • Make fast decisions: save, dismiss, or revisit
  • Build a short reading queue instead of an endless backlog

Key Ideas

Keep up with new papers: StrataFeed is built for regular scanning—quickly see what is new without getting buried.

Explore topics: Use discovery as a research method: map an area, find key authors, and follow emerging subfields.

Powered by OpenAlex: StrataFeed uses the OpenAlex open scholarly catalog as its metadata backbone.

Calm by default: Minimal interface and fast triage so you spend time reading only what matters.
