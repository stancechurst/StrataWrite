What the suite is for

Research software for people who already have a serious writing practice.

StrataSuite avoids the everything-app trap. It keeps the reading surface, note network, and manuscript surface distinct enough to stay usable.

Structure-first thinking

Use links, tags, categories, and outlines to keep arguments from dissolving into a pile of fragments.

Apple-native polish

SwiftUI, CloudKit, native text systems, and platform conventions instead of a wrapped web interface.

Research privacy

Your notes, manuscripts, PDFs, and annotations stay on device or in your private iCloud account if sync is enabled.
