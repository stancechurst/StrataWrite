Privacy Policy for StrataWrite

Last updated: September 2025

StrataWrite is a research‑first writing app for macOS and iPadOS. Protecting your privacy is important. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is used.

Data Collection

StrataWrite does not collect, track, or transmit personal data to the developer. Your notes, manuscripts, and writing data remain stored locally on your device.

iCloud and Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored securely in your personal iCloud account. Apple manages the encryption, transmission, and storage of this data. The StrataWrite developer does not have access to your iCloud data.

Analytics

StrataWrite does not include third‑party analytics, advertising, or tracking software.

Support

If you contact support at stance@mac.com, any information you share in that email will be used solely to respond to your request.

Your Rights

You retain full control of your data. You may delete your notes and manuscripts at any time. If you disable iCloud sync, related data stored in iCloud is removed according to Apple’s policies.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact: stance@mac.com
