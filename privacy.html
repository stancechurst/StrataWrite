Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to StrataWrite, StrataRead, and StrataFeed (collectively referred to as StrataSuite), research-focused applications for macOS and iPadOS. Protecting your privacy is important. This policy explains what data these apps handle and how it is used.

Data Collection

StrataWrite, StrataRead, and StrataFeed do not collect, track, sell, or transmit personal data to the developer.

All user-created or user-managed content—including notes, manuscripts, PDFs, annotations, and reading lists—remains stored locally on your device or within your personal iCloud account if sync is enabled.

iCloud and Sync

If you enable iCloud sync in any StrataSuite app, your data is stored securely in your personal iCloud account. Apple manages the encryption, transmission, and storage of this data. The developer does not have access to your iCloud-stored content.

External Data Sources

StrataFeed retrieves publicly available scholarly metadata (such as article titles, authors, abstracts, and publication information) from the OpenAlex open scholarly catalog. These requests do not include personal identifiers and are not used for user tracking or profiling.

Analytics and Tracking

StrataSuite apps do not include third-party analytics, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies.

Support Communications

If you contact support at stance@mac.com, any information you provide in that email will be used solely to respond to your request and will not be shared or retained for other purposes.

Your Rights and Control

You retain full control of your data. You may delete notes, manuscripts, PDFs, annotations, and reading data directly within the apps at any time.

If iCloud sync is enabled, related data stored in iCloud can be managed or deleted through your Apple ID iCloud settings in accordance with Apple’s policies.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact: stance@mac.com
